This calculator is intended for preliminary estimate purposes only for Nassau County, Florida, US. Calculator is provided as a courtesy and can be used as a guideline to estimate the approximate amount of Mobility Fees that may be due for a given project. There are many factors that may affect the total fees. Actual trip generation and Mobility Fees will be ultimately determined by the County Development Services. Additionally, many developments include multiple uses, and each use should be calculated individually and combined for the estimated total fee. The prime idea behind NC Mobility Fee Calculator was to create a platform which would allow users to calculate their Mobility Fee based on the type of property, its Land-use code, what units it offers and calculate the the fee dynamically to give an estimate of what the fee might be. It takes into account multiple factors like fixed-zone values, pass-by-percentage etc. Complex mathematical formulas are implemented at the back-end for successful completion of the project and avoid even a single digit error.
