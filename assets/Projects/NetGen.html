NETGEN delivers demonstration places for forestry information exchange and emphasizes the value of DNA and Co. in the forest.


Healthy and stable forests are required for the forest to perform its many responsibilities. They include natural disaster prevention, timber production, biodiversity preservation, and drinking water and air filtration. The healthier a tree is, the better it adapts to the natural circumstances in its site.


Project involved a mixture of mapping technologies and used Mapbox, Leaflet & leaflet routing for calculation of the route. User would select a location on the map and it would display the hiking route with individual station points along with the total distance etc.
