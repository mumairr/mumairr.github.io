Climate change had its impact globally on all aspects of life. Experts across the domain are continually investigating new technologies and working practices to keep track of the changes and their associated impacts on the enviroment. That's why Pakistan Drought Management System is developed, to tie together the newest technologies with expert insight to help users run, learn and devise socio economic based decisions following affects of climate change.


The central idea behind PakDMS was to create a platform which could host, manage and inform, rather than just store data, putting the user in control where user can filter list of inidices based on mutiple criteria like geometry (administrative boundary), dates, aggregation to be performed etc.


This was a consultancy project, where IWMI (Inernational Water Management) is the client. It was a team effort where indices were handled by respective consultants and I was the lead developer for the portal. My prime responsibility in the task was to design, develop & deploy complete portal on IWMI server, integrate data from python based Google Earth Engine codebase, Geo-visualization of the layers, filtering etc. User could either preview the layers on the map geologically or view its tabular data in the form of graph.

Portal allows user to switch between themes, uses latest available libraries like Antd & Material UI for different components and icons.
